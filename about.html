About MaryJane Careers

Once upon a time, in a sunny corner of the world, three lifelong friends—Lila, Mari, and Sage—sat around a kitchen table, sipping tea and dreaming about how they could make a difference. Each of them had their own unique story: Lila was a spirited HR professional with a knack for spotting hidden talent, Mari was a passionate advocate for fair labor practices, and Sage was an ever-curious entrepreneur who always had her finger on the pulse of emerging industries.

Their conversation turned to the cannabis industry, a field they all admired for its potential to heal and empower communities. But they couldn’t ignore the stories they’d heard—of employees working tirelessly for unfair wages and companies struggling to find dedicated workers.

“What if we could change that?” Sage asked, her eyes sparkling with possibility.

And so, their dream began to bloom. The three friends founded "Higher Ground Careers", a company dedicated to connecting people with jobs in the cannabis industry while ensuring fair pay and ethical working conditions.

Lila poured her heart into creating a platform that matched workers with roles perfectly suited to their skills and passions. Mari championed initiatives to educate employers about building inclusive and respectful workplaces. Meanwhile, Sage built relationships with local businesses, turning every handshake into a partnership.

What made their company truly special, though, was the warmth they brought to their work. Job seekers loved how Lila took the time to learn their stories. Employers valued Mari’s dedication to fairness. And everyone was inspired by Sage’s vision for a more compassionate industry.

Their office became a hub of laughter, hope, and possibility. Every week, they held "Coffee & Conversations" with job seekers and employers, turning professional networking into a celebration of community. They even hosted workshops on resume-building, interview prep, and the art of negotiating a salary—all with a playful twist, like raffles for "Best Dressed Resume" or giveaways of tiny potted cannabis plants.

Over time, Higher Ground Careers became more than a business—it became a movement. They didn’t just fill positions; they nurtured careers, friendships, and futures. And in doing so, they proved that you could build a thriving company rooted in kindness, integrity, and a deep belief in the power of people.

To this day, Lila, Mari, and Sage still gather around that same kitchen table, now a little more worn and covered in notes and doodles. Over tea, they dream of the next way they’ll make the world a little better—one job, one person, and one smile at a time.
